About

Operator. Builder. SEO architect.

I'm Kyle McCool, Co-Founder and Director of Operations at Makko AI, an AI-native 2D game studio. I own go-to-market, content, SEO, brand, and operations end-to-end. I led the rebrand from Snib.ai to Makko AI in 2025 and built the company's growth engine in-house with no external agencies.

Previously spent four years (2021 to 2025) at Moonray PBC as Marketing Manager, scaling community to 170,000+ followers and contributing to the largest NFT mint in OpenSea history at 220,000 assets.

Outside Makko, I co-founded Phantomline (local-first AI video studio) and ProLocalBuilder (flat-fee websites for local service businesses) with Cesar Martinez and Zac Dillard.
